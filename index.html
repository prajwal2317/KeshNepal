About Kesh Nepal

At Kesh Nepal, we believe a small act of kindness can make a world of difference. We bring people together through hair donation campaigns across Nepal—connecting those willing to give with those who’ve lost their hair to cancer or other medical conditions. For someone going through a tough time, a donated wig can mean so much more than just hair—it can bring back confidence, hope, and a smile.

Why Donate Hair?

Support Cancer Patients

Your hair donation helps make wigs for individuals who have lost their hair during treatment.

Build Confidence

Donated wigs help restore dignity and self-esteem to those in need.

Create Impact

Even a small gesture like hair donation can have a huge impact on someone’s life.
